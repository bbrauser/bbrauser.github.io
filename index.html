About Me
Based in Murray, KY, I am a data science professional with a background as unique as it is robust. I graduated from Murray State University in 2010 with a Bachelor's of Science in Music, a degree that instilled in me a profound appreciation for the harmonies and patterns that also exist in the world of data. After pivoting my career, I completed the Data Science program at the Bloom Institute of Technology (formerly Lambda School) in 2020 and am currently working toward a Master's of Science in Information Systems with a concentration in Business Analytics, set to graduate in December 2023. My goal is to apply my proficiency in Python, R, SQL, Excel, Google Sheets, Tableau, SPSS Statistics, IBM SPSS Modeler, and RapidMiner Studio to help businesses make data-driven decisions that pave the way for their success.
